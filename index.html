Privacy Policy for Speed Tunes

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Speed Tunes ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

We collect your device's location data to provide speed tracking and ride logging features. This includes real-time GPS data while the app is in use or running in the background.

Location data is used only for app functionality and is not transmitted or shared with third parties.

2. Bluetooth Data

We use Bluetooth to connect to compatible devices such as VESC controllers and external GPS units. This allows the app to read speed and telemetry data.

No Bluetooth data is transmitted off your device.

3. Local Storage

Ride history, analytics, and user settings are stored locally on your device using system storage (such as AsyncStorage / NSUserDefaults).

This data is not uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location history on remote servers
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts or personal identifiers

How We Use Information

We use the collected data solely to provide core app functionality, including:

  • Speed tracking
  • Audio feedback based on speed
  • Ride analytics and history

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data.

Permissions

Speed Tunes may request the following permissions:

  • Location: Required for speed tracking and ride recording
  • Bluetooth: Required to connect to external devices
  • Background Activity: Required to continue tracking while the app is not actively open

Your Rights

You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
